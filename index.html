For Pihu

Pihu,
on this day, the sky unties a ribbon of light,
and every star leans closer
just to wish you before I do.

Bacche aap har saal itne sundar hote jaa rahe ho,
jaise maano ye dharti saavan mein khilte jaa rahi hai,
har pal aap mere life ko enlighten kardete ho.

Each candle I light carries a secret—
that your laughter has been my brightest flame,
and every year, you burn brighter than before.

Aaj aapka janmdin hai—
aur main chahta hoon duniya thoda dheere chale,
taaki main aapko aur der tak dekh pau,
aur aapko aur gehraai se kissi de pau.

You are not just another year older,
you are another year of magic,
another verse in the poem I never stop writing,
another wish I never stop making.

the centerpiece, with all my love

If the moon had dimples, it would copy yours.
If hours had wings, they'd land on your laugh.

for Pihu, on your day

Main har subah aapke sapne lekar uth ta hu
aur shaam hote hi aapki yaad wapas aane lagti hai.

for Pihu, on your day

Another year around the sun, and still—
my favorite orbit is the one where I revolve around you.
